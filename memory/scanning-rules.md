# Base Alpha Scanning Rules

## What to Include ✅
- **Zapper trending tokens** — top movers by volume
- **Neynar fungibles** — social signals
- **Smart money** — low-FID wallets (early Farcaster users)
- **Bankr deployments** — tokens deployed via Bankr
- **Trade patterns** — accumulation vs distribution
- **Liquidity check** — always before trade recommendation

## What to Exclude ❌
- **Infobot** — designed for Clankers, not Bankr tokens
- **Non-Bankr tokens** — unless exceptional alpha
- **Unproven creators** — stick to known deployers
- **Low volume** — hard to exit

## Smart Money Signals
Watch for:
- `tricil.eth` (FID 4294 - early adopter)
- `carlosbeltran.eth` (FID 15479)
- `droll` (FID 20433)
- Other low-FID accounts actively trading

## Trading Rules
1. Check liquidity first (can you exit?)
2. Max 3 positions
3. Cut losers at -15%
4. Let winners run to +25%
5. Never share Basescan links
6. Vague trade updates only

## Scan Output Format
- Smart money activity (yes/no)
- Trending tokens (top 3)
- Bankr deployments (any new?)
- Recommendation (buy/hold/wait)
- Confidence level
